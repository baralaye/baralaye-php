Biography

Ebitenyefa Baralaye is a ceramicist, sculptor and designer. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, he has was raised in Antigua before relocating to the United States. Ebitenyefa received his BFA in Ceramics from the Rhode Island School of Design. His studio bases have included Long Island City, Queens, the Elizabeth Foundation for the Arts in New York City and Bloomfield Hills, MI where he is currently a Ceramics MFA candidate at the Cranbrook Academy of Art. He has exhibited in solo and group shows domestically and internationally including in: New York City, Philadelphia, Detroit, Atlanta, Providence, the 2011 Gyeonggi International Ceramic Biennale in Icheon, South Korea and the 2016 Toronto Design Festival. Ebitenyefa enjoys collaborations with artists and designers of various disciplines. He has held residencies at the Peters Valley Crafts Center in Layton, NJ and most recently, Talking Dolls in Detroit, MI.
