Biography

Ebitenyefa Baralaye is a sculptor, mixed media artist and designer. He was born in Lagos, Nigeria and lives across the United States. Ebitenyefa received a BFA in Ceramics from the Rhode Island School of Design (2006) and an MFA in Ceramics from the Cranbrook Academy of Art (2016, expected). Ebitenyefa has held residencies at the Peters Valley Crafts Center in Layton, NJ, The Elizabeth Foundation for the Arts in NYC, and Talking Dolls in Detroit, MI. He has exhibited in various solo and group shows domestically and internationally including the 2011 Gyeonggi International Ceramix Biennale in Icheon, South Korea. He is currently represented by David Klein Gallery in Detroit.
